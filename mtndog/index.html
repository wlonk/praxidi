Making Teams Better.

We design immersive educational experiences that feel like games. No lectures. No slideshows. Your team will learn strategy and communication by taking chances, seeing what works, and fixing mistakes. At Praxidi, we believe that learning is always about doing.

Learn more

Game-based workshops

Our game-based model stands out from the competition because it is intrinsically motivating. You win or lose based on how quickly you learn the same skills you need at work. Meaningful but low stakes mean you have more emotional buy-in so you remember what you learned. And it’s fun.

Leadership

  • Test your leadership style under high-stress situations to see where you need work
  • Learn how everyone on a team can and should be a leader (without stepping on each other’s toes)
  • Distinguish between decision-making, organizing, and leading
  • Recognize when to step up and when to step back and let others take the lead
  • Refine your personal leadership style

Communication

  • Identify and eliminate communication bottlenecks
  • Develop team jargon that is more efficient (and fun)
  • Improve your listening skills under stressful, time-intensive situations

Teamwork

  • Develop a ‘team intuition’ to know what the team needs automatically
  • Shift in and out of team roles seamlessly
  • Master conflict resolution in the team
